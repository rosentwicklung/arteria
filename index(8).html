How It Works

The Arteria cycle — from concept to culture
REGISTER

Upload

Artists register original work on Arteria. Physical and digital — every piece gets a permanent record. The originating artist retains full royalties on all derivative works.

EVOLVE

Collaborate

Curated artists can propose evolution projects — digitalisations, sound layers, physical editions, experience layers. Every branch requires artist approval.

FUND

Vote

The community funds projects through VENA — the platform's internal credit. Stake VENA to support proposals. Collectors earn from projects they believe in early.

SPOTLIGHT

Amplify

Weekly spotlight selects one artist or project for featured placement. Entry via VENA raffle. The community curates — not algorithms, not galleries.

Apply for Access

All applications are reviewed by the Arteria curatorial team. Artists are onboarded based on work quality and alignment. Collectors and investors open on approval.

All applications are reviewed within 5–7 days. You will be contacted via email. Arteria reserves the right to decline applications. Home and Apply are open to everyone — login is required for Portfolio, Workspace, and Artelier.

How it works
· 10 VENA is deducted from circulation (deflationary)
· 10 VENA enters the curator pool
· Eligible accounts may enter up to 3× per week — extra entries increase selection weight
· Entries from unverified or inactive accounts are not counted, to keep the pool resistant to mass fake-account abuse
· Winner announced every Monday 00:00 UTC
